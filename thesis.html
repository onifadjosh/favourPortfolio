Thesis Archive

Thesis Archive is a digital repository designed to simplify the way academic institutions store and access final-year research papers. The platform empowers students and faculty members to upload, search, and download thesis projects across multiple departments and academic years. With an intuitive interface and smart filters, it encourages collaboration, improves knowledge sharing, and preserves academic work in a well-organized system.

Live Link

Problem Statement

In many tertiary institutions, access to previous research work is limited, disorganized, or entirely offline. Students often struggle to find relevant past thesis papers for reference, and lecturers lack a centralized system for reviewing or showcasing academic progress. This disconnect leads to repetitive research topics, lost academic insights, and underutilization of valuable intellectual work

Solution

The solution was to develop a simple and accessible digital platform where students can upload their final-year thesis and browse previously submitted ones. Lecturers also have the ability to review and monitor students' thesis submissions. Overview

Thesis Archive is a web-based platform designed to streamline how academic thesis papers are submitted, accessed, and reviewed. It was built to solve the common issue of disorganized, hard-to-access final-year projects in many institutions. The platform allows students to upload their thesis directly, while lecturers can review submissions in one centralized space. By digitizing the process, Thesis Archive helps preserve academic work, encourages accountability, and supports a more efficient supervision system.

Role

Product Designer

UX Process

  1. Research & Discovery: Conducted informal interviews with students and lecturers to understand pain points in thesis submission and access.
    • Identified key challenges: manual documentation, lack of access to past work, and unclear supervision flow.
  2. User Flow Mapping: Created simple flows for students (upload/view thesis) and lecturers (review access).
    • Focused on making the platform minimal and distraction-free.
  3. Wireframing: Sketched out low-fidelity wireframes to plan structure and layout.
    • Created a consistent design system to maintain visual harmony across all screens.
  4. High-Fidelity Design: Designed a clean UI using Figma with clear sections for all key actions.
    • Prioritized typography and spacing to ensure readability and simplicity.
  5. Prototype & Feedback: Built interactive prototypes and shared with test users.
    • Gathered insights and made final adjustments.
